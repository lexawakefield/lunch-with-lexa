What I'm up to

I'm rediscovering my passion for fitness training. Pursuing my CPT with NASM and a Group Fitness Certification with AFAA. About to start teaching F45 classes, and I used to instruct cycling — so stepping back into a gym feels a little like coming home.

A lot of this comeback is happening because I had to quit running, which I love. I have a genetic condition, and there's a good chance I'll need a major surgery that would mean learning to walk again.

So instead of letting it flatten me, I'm finding joy in new movement and getting as strong as I possibly can.

What's next

I'm working through nutrition certifications too. My dream is to build an independent fitness coaching business that helps people reclaim their agency, through movement, nutrition, and mindset. I'm especially passionate about helping people break through their limiting beliefs.

I believe our bodies are the best tools we have to teach our brains new skills.
